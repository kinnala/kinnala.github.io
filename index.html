1 Introduction

I'm an applied mathematician focusing on numerical methods and scientific computing. I'm currently employed by Aalto University, Department of Mathematics and Systems Analysis as a postdoctoral researcher, working in the Academy of Finland project Effektiva finita element metoder inom kontinuummekanik, 2019–22. I'm also developing machine learning software as a part time Data Scientist at Eniram Oy since January 2019.

2 Research

In 2018, I finished my D.Sc. dissertation on Finite Element Methods for Contact Problems which was funded mainly by the Finnish Cultural Foundation. You can find my articles in Orcid and Google Scholar. I often upload preprints to arXiv.

In the past, I have volunteered to work as a peer reviewer for the following scientific journals:

  • Mathematics and Mechanics of Solids, 2020
  • ESAIM: Mathematical Modelling and Numerical Analysis, 2020
  • Proceedings of the Royal Society A, 2020
  • BIT Numerical Mathematics, 2020
  • Computational Methods in Applied Mathematics, 2018
  • Rakenteiden Mekaniikka, 2018, 2017
  • Applied Mathematics and Computation, 2015
